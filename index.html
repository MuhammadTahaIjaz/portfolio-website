MealConnect: End to End Meal Ordering Platform

Schools and cafeterias had no connected system for meal ordering, payments, or delivery tracking. Parents had no visibility, cafeteria staff managed everything manually, and school admins had no oversight of approvals or performance. Built MealConnect, a three panel connected system: a Flutter Android app for parents to register, add children, browse cafeteria menus, and pay through Stripe; a separate Flutter Android app for cafeteria staff to manage listings, view active orders, and receive automatic Stripe Express payouts on delivery confirmation; and a Flutter Web admin panel for school administrators to verify accounts, approve registrations, and track monthly delivery performance through real time charts. Every approved child receives a unique MealConnect ID used by cafeteria staff for order lookup at fulfillment. The entire architecture is multi school, meaning each admin sees only data from their own institution.

📱 Three panel system across parents, cafeteria staff & admins 💳 Automated Stripe Express payouts on delivery confirmation 🏫 Multi school architecture with complete data isolation
